About Us

Surface cargo specialists with a pan-India network

We are a Pune-based surface cargo and logistics company committed to moving what matters — reliably, efficiently, and on time. Founded with a clear mission to bridge the gap between businesses and their customers across India, we specialise in full-scale surface freight solutions covering both high-density urban corridors and deep last-mile ODA (Out of Delivery Area) pincodes.

Headquartered in Kothrud, Pune, our network spans over 22,000 pincodes across India — from metro fulfilment hubs to remote district towns. We partner with leading logistics carriers to ensure every consignment, regardless of size or destination, is handled with transparency, care, and accountability. Trusted by clients including Piaggio Vehicles Pvt. Ltd., we bring the same operational discipline to every shipment.
